## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What is Carnatic Metronome?**  
A: Carnatic Metronome is a professional metronome and accompaniment app designed specifically for Carnatic music practice and performance. It provides accurate rhythm practice with traditional tala patterns, shruthi (pitch) generation, and mridangam accompaniment.

**Q: What talas are supported?**  
A: The app supports multiple Carnatic talas including:
- Adi Tala (8 beats)
- Rupaka Tala (3 beats)
- Misra Chapu (7 beats)
- Khanda Chapu (5 beats)
- Eka Tala (4 beats)
- Kanda Triputa (9 beats)
- Ata Tala (14 beats)
- Sankeerna Chapu (9 beats)
- Jhampa Tala (6 beats)

**Q: What nadais are available?**  
A: The app supports various nadais (subdivisions):
- Chatusra (4)
- Tisra (3)
- Khanda (5)
- Misra (7)
- Sankeerna (9)

**Q: What pitch options are available?**  
A: You can select from multiple shruthi (pitch) options: C, C#, D, E, F, G, G#, A, B.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: What tempo range is supported?**  
A: The app supports tempo variations from 20-200 BPM, depending on the selected tala and available audio files.

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**  
A: Yes, the app requires an internet connection to stream audio files from AWS S3. Audio files are not stored locally on the device.

**Q: Does the app use the microphone?**  
A: The app may request microphone permission for optional real-time pitch detection (stayi detection) feature. This feature can be disabled if you prefer not to use it.

**Q: What data does the app collect?**  
A: The app processes audio locally on your device for pitch detection (if enabled). Audio data is not stored or transmitted to external servers. The app streams audio files from AWS S3 for mridangam accompaniment, but does not collect personal information.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is not playing audio**  
A: 
- Check your device volume
- Ensure you have an internet connection
- Try restarting the app
- Check that the selected tala, nadai, and tempo combination has available audio files

**Q: Audio is choppy or delayed**  
A:
- Check your internet connection speed
- Try selecting a different tempo
- Close other apps that may be using bandwidth

**Q: The shruthi generator is not working**  
A:
- Check your device volume
- Ensure the shruthi volume slider is not set to zero
- Try selecting a different pitch

**Q: Beat indicators are not showing correctly**  
A:
- Ensure you've selected a valid tala
- Check that the app has loaded successfully
- Try restarting the app
